
# Scambo: Safe Exchanges Between People

## What is Scambo?

Scambo is an application that allows people to exchange products and services with each other in a safe and reliable way. Imagine wanting to trade guitar lessons for English lessons, or a smartphone for a bicycle, without the risk of being scammed!

## Who is it for?

For anyone who wants to make exchanges without using money, whether physical objects or services like lessons, repairs, or creative work.

## How it works (step by step):

### 1. Simple and secure registration

- You create an account with email and phone
- The app verifies that you are a real person
- For higher-value exchanges, the app may request a photo of you and a document

### 2. Post what you offer

- You photograph the product or describe the service
- Add a short video to prove the item exists or that you know how to provide the service
- Define approximately how much it's worth (for reference)

### 3. Exchange proposal

- Another person finds your offer and proposes an exchange
- You chat through the app's built-in chat
- If both agree, the proposal is formalized

### 4. Guarantee system

- Both parties deposit a "symbolic amount" in the app as guarantee
- This deposit is only returned when the exchange is successful
- This discourages ill-intentioned people

### 5. Meeting and exchange

- You arrange date and location (or link, if online)
- Perform the exchange
- Each confirms in the app that they received what was agreed
- If everything goes well, the deposit is returned and you gain positive reputation

### 6. If there are problems

- Anyone can open a complaint with evidence (photos, messages)
- The app analyzes and decides who is right
- The deposited guarantee can be used to compensate whoever was harmed

## Main ideas:

### Layered security

The app creates multiple barriers against scams:

- Identity verification (basic for everyone, more complete for valuable exchanges)
- Visual proof of items/services
- Financial guarantee system (deposit)
- User ratings after each exchange

### Progressive trust

- New users start by being able to exchange only lower-value items
- As they make successful exchanges, they gain access to larger exchanges
- This protects the community from fraudsters

### Conflict mediation

Instead of "every man for himself," the app offers:

- Complaint system with evidence
- Analysis by real people
- Compensation when necessary

## Differentials:

- **No real money circulating**: unlike traditional marketplaces, the focus is on direct exchange
- **Guarantee without complexity**: the deposit system is simple but effective against scams
- **Risk-appropriate verification**: the higher the exchange value, the more verification is required
- **Mandatory visual evidence**: photos and videos that prove the existence of the item/service
- **Reputation that really matters**: your history determines what you can do in the app

In summary, Scambo brings to the digital world the trust necessary to make exchanges with strangers, solving the problem of "how to trust someone I've never seen before?", allowing people to better leverage the resources they already possess.
